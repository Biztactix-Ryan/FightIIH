About IIH Explained

Providing clear, accessible information about Idiopathic Intracranial Hypertension to help patients, families, and caregivers understand this complex condition.

Common Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Vision Changes
  • Pulsatile Tinnitus
  • Eye Pain
  • Neck & Shoulder Pain
  • Brain Fog
  • Dizziness
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea

Treatment Options

  • Medications
  • Lumbar Puncture
  • Weight Management
  • Lifestyle Changes
  • Shunt Surgery
  • Venous Stenting
  • ONSF Surgery
  • Pain Management
  • Complementary Therapies
