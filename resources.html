Intracranial Hypertension Research Foundation (IHRF)

The only non-profit organization in the world devoted to supporting medical research of chronic intracranial hypertension. Provides education, support, and research funding.

United Kingdom

IIH UK

UK charity with an extremely supportive online group of over 5,000 members. Provides information, support, and advocacy for IIH patients.

Visit IIH UK Research Section
Australia

IH Australia

Dedicated to providing information and support to people with IIH in Australia and New Zealand. Offers support groups on Facebook and awareness merchandise.

Visit IH Australia
United Kingdom

The Brain Charity

Supports people with all neurological conditions including IIH. Offers practical help, emotional support, counselling, and social activities from their Liverpool centre.
